Manage your home energy use with a smart thermostat.
Heating and cooling can make up nearly half of your home's energy use. With an ENERGY STAR® certified smart thermostat, you can save without sacrificing comfort. A smart thermostat can learn your schedule and adjust automatically, so you don't even have to think about it—or you can easily take control from your phone, tablet, or computer.
SMECO makes upgrading easy too. Whether you want to install it yourself or get help from a pro, SMECO offers many ways for members to get a smart thermostat. Some are even available at no additional cost.
